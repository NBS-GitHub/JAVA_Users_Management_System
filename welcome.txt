Welcome to the latest version of the Users Management System - UMS.

Create a UMS by instantiating the UsersSystem class.
As a UMS administrator, you have a wide range of useful methods at your disposal.
These are the methods available at each UsersSystem instance:

- addUser(String firstName, String lastName, boolean hasPremium),
- removeUser(int id),
- showFirstName(int id),
- changeFirstName(int id, String newFirstName),
- showLastName(int id),
- changeLastName(int id, String newLastName),
- showPremiumStatus(int id),
- changePremiumStatus(int id),
- showUserInfo(int id),
- showUsersCount().
